**ChatLens AI** imports your Telegram Desktop chat export and rebuilds it into a fast,
fully **offline** archive you actually own — instant search, a media library, link
intelligence, people analytics and AI-powered insights. Nothing is uploaded. There is no
account and no tracking. Your conversations never leave your PC.

## 🔒 Privacy first

ChatLens AI is **offline-first by design**. It makes no network requests unless you
explicitly opt in, AI runs locally, and your archive stays on your machine. See the
[Privacy Policy](PRIVACY.md).

## ❓ FAQ

**Does ChatLens upload my data?** No. It is offline-first; your data stays on your device.

**How do I export from Telegram?** Telegram Desktop → Settings → Advanced →
Export Telegram data → choose **JSON** format, then import that folder in ChatLens AI.

**Is Pro a subscription?** No — Pro is a **one-time purchase** with a lifetime licence and
all future updates included.
