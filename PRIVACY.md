# Privacy Policy — ChatLens AI

_Last updated: 2026-06-20_

ChatLens AI ("the app") is published by **Cybesis Studios**. This policy explains what the
app does and does not do with your data.

## The short version

**ChatLens AI is offline-first. Your data never leaves your device.** There is no account,
no sign-in, no analytics, and no tracking.

## What data the app processes

- **Your Telegram exports.** When you import a Telegram Desktop export, the app reads the
  JSON and media files and stores a local index in your Windows user profile
  (`%LOCALAPPDATA%\com.cybesis.chatlens`). This data stays on your computer.

## What we collect

- **Nothing.** The app does not transmit your messages, media, contacts, or usage to
  Cybesis Studios or any third party. The app makes no network requests unless you
  explicitly enable an optional online feature (e.g. fetching link previews), which is
  **off by default**.

## Purchases

- The free/Pro purchase is handled entirely by the **Microsoft Store**. We never see your
  payment details. Entitlement is verified through the Store's licensing API on your device.

## Data deletion

- Deleting a vault in the app removes its local index. Uninstalling the app removes all app
  data. Your original Telegram export files are never modified.
